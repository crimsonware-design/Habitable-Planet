Gliese 667 Cc

Pros Cons
Earth-like You would weigh more
Good temperature Their Star is active (Solar flares, etc)

How could we colonize it: This planet is believed to be in its habitable zone for the stars so we would not need to worry about temperature. The parent star is known to be quite active, with frequent flares that could be dangerous to any life on this planet, you would also weigh more due to the planet having more mass.
