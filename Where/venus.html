Venus

Pros Cons
Near earth Thick, Toxic atmosphere
Lots of energy we could make from atmosphere Hottest planet in the solar system (842°F)

How could we colonize it: Venus is extremely inhospitable with a thick, toxic atmosphere and a surface temperature hot enough to melt lead. The most proposed ideas for colonizing Venus are by building sky Bases using aerostats (any type of aircraft that generates lift by using floating gas) or floating platforms so that we do not have to go to the surface. If we get there it could be useful to mine resources, to be self-sustainable.
