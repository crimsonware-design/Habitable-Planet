Pros Cons
In habitable zone 49 lightyears away
Earth-like surface We know barely anything about it

How would we colonize it: Gliese 163c is 49 lightyears away so much too far for us to travel currently. Scientists believe that it has a rocky surface and is in the habitable zone of its star, meaning it could potentially have liquid water. We do not know what the atmosphere is like, so we do not know if it is toxic or not.
